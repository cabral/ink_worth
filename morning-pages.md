# Morning Pages

This is the full guide, still being written. For now, here is the short version.

Morning Pages come from Julia Cameron's *The Artist's Way*. The practice is three pages, handwritten, first thing in the morning. No editing, no reading back, no audience. You write whatever is in your head until three pages are full, then you stop.

The guide will cover three things:

- How to start, including what you actually need (a notebook and a pen) and what you don't.
- What to expect in the first few weeks, when the writing tends to feel pointless before it stops feeling that way.
- What happens when you skip days, and why missing a morning is not a reason to quit.

Until then, the practice is the same whether or not the guide exists. Open the notebook tomorrow morning and fill three pages.
